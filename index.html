Frequently asked questions

We’ve answered your top questions to show how we can transform your brand.

What types of brands do you work with?

We specialize in working with luxury brands that prioritize quality, elegance, and distinction. Our expertise spans industries such as beauty, lifestyle, hospitality, fashion, and premium services.

How long does the branding process take?

Our branding process typically takes 6-8 weeks, depending on the scope and complexity of the project. We begin with an in-depth discovery phase, followed by strategy development, design, and final delivery. You’ll receive regular updates throughout the process, ensuring transparency and a collaborative experience from start to finish.

Do you offer custom branding packages?

Yes, all our branding packages are fully customizable to meet your unique needs and goals. Whether you’re looking for a full brand identity, storytelling, or specific deliverables like logos and guidelines, we tailor our services to ensure the final result aligns with your vision and elevates your brand.

Will I receive all the files needed for my brand?

Absolutely! At the end of the project, you’ll receive a comprehensive suite of files, including high-resolution logo files, brand guidelines, color palettes, typography specifications, and any additional assets created for your brand. We ensure you have everything needed to maintain consistency across all touchpoints.

What makes Timeless different from other branding agencies?

Our dedication to luxury branding sets us apart. We focus on crafting designs that are timeless, blending sophisticated aesthetics with strategic storytelling to create a deep connection with your audience. With meticulous attention to detail and a collaborative approach, we ensure your brand stands out in even the most competitive markets.
